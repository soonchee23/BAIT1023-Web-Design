ABOUT US





Mars Café


Our cafe is inspired by the universe. The founder is a person who cannot fulfill his dream to become an astronaut. He is also a food lover who likes to do research on the recipe of the food, especially local food. He is planning to open cafes with different planet themes. This is the first cafe which has a Mars theme. That is the reason it is called "Mars Cafe". He hopes that everyone that visits here will be able to feel that they are enjoying their meals on Mars and provide a space to let every universe lover interact with each other. Besides that, the founder also paid a lot of attention to the decoration of the cafe.He also made sure that the cafe has all interesting fun facts about the universe which are able to draw the attention of the customers. He hopes that every visitor is able to take some unique pictures here and post it on social media.
